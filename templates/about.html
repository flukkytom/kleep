About Kleep

Kleep was built to help creators, educators, and entrepreneurs easily turn long-form videos into viral-ready short clips.

Using smart AI analysis, Kleep finds the **most powerful moments** in your videos — then automatically kleeps (clips) and formats them for TikTok, Instagram, YouTube Shorts, and more.

Whether you're uploading a fresh video or dropping a YouTube link, Kleep saves you hours of manual editing.

It's time to let your best moments shine ✨.
